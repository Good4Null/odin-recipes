Asgard Big Mac Supreme

Description

When the mortal world just won’t cut it, summon the flavors of Valhalla with this towering Asgard Big Mac Supreme. Layers of seasoned beef, melty cheese, tangy pickles, and secret sauce unite in a golden-baked casserole that could satisfy even Odin after a long day of ruling the Nine Realms. It’s bold, it’s cheesy, and it might just earn you a spot at the feasting tables of Asgard.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef (96/4)
  • 1 lb russet potatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 white onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp. olive oil
  • 2 tsp. yellow mustard
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. onion powder
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, 112g

Asgard Sauce

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 55g
  • 1/4 cup ketchup, 65g
  • 1/4 cup dill pickles, chopped
  • 1 tbsp. dill pickle juice

Feast Finishers

  • 1 vine tomato, chopped
  • handful white onions, chopped
  • handful shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 1/4 cup dill pickle chips

Instructions

  1. Stoke the Fires of Asgard

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C), as if lighting the forges of the gods.

    Wash and chop your potatoes into small, bite-sized pieces (skins on if you wish for rustic glory). Smaller pieces will crisp faster in battle.

  2. Sear the Potatoes of Midgard

    Heat a sturdy pan over medium flame with a drizzle of golden olive oil.

    Add the potatoes and ¼ cup water, seasoning generously with salt and pepper.

    Stir often to prevent scorching until they soften like weary warriors (about 5–10 minutes, depending on size).

    Transfer these potato stones to your baking dish.

  3. Combine the Feast of Warriors

    In the same pan, add ground beef and onions.

    Cook until the beef is mostly conquered by flame and the onions are soft.

    Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and mustard, stirring well to unite the flavors.

    Add this savory legion to the potatoes and mix them together.

  4. Crown your Casserole

    Shower the mixture with shredded cheddar, the golden treasure of the mortal realms.

    Cover with foil like a shining shield and bake for 15 minutes, until the cheese melts into molten glory.

  5. Conjure the Sauce of Asgard

    While it bakes, craft your sacred sauce: combine mayo, ketchup, chopped pickles, and a splash of pickle brine, stirring until smooth.

    Chill this divine elixir in the fridge until it’s time to anoint your creation.

  6. Finish the Casserole

    Remove the foil shield and drizzle the Asgard Sauce in generous streaks — leave none behind, for the gods are generous.

    Crown your feast with shredded lettuce, chopped white onion, diced tomato, and full pickle chips.

    Behold: your Asgard Big Mac Supreme, worthy of the halls of Valhalla.
